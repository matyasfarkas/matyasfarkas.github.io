External forecasts

Bring in an outside forecast as an ingredient — a pin fed into the model's Kalman smoother with an uncertainty you set. The slider runs from Ext forecast (zero uncertainty — pinned exactly) to BVAR (infinite uncertainty — the pure model); in between, the two are blended and every other variable responds. Imported points show as teal diamonds.
